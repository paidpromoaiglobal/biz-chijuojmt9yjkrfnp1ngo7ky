About Us

Your Preferred Stay in Indore

Hotel Pavilion is a well-loved hospitality destination in the heart of Vijay Nagar, Indore — conveniently located behind Malhar Mega Mall, one of the city's premier shopping centres.

We pride ourselves on delivering warm, personalised service that keeps our guests coming back. Whether you're visiting Indore for business or leisure, our clean, comfortable rooms and attentive staff ensure a stay you'll remember fondly.
